Lemon Shrimp Pasta

Admittedly, though I might have just finished dinner, a single glace at this picture has made me hungry again. Oh no! I guess I have no choice but to go ahead with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 package fresh linguine(or whatever pasta you prefer) pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup freshly grated parsesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Coat shrimp with salt and pepper. Cook and stir shrimp in the hot oil until opaque, about 2 minutes per side.
  3. Cook linguine in the boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, 1 to 3 minutes.
  4. Combine lemon zest and juice in a small saucepan over medium heat and bring to a simmer. Sprinkle in parsley and stir in butter until melted. Add 2 tablespoons of the pasta cooking water and stir. Drain pasta. Add shrimp to the lemon sauce. Mix pasta, shrimp, sauce, and Parmesan cheese together in a large serving bowl.
